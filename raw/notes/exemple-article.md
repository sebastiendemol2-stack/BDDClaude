# Why Context Beats Prompting

Source: hypothetical blog post for demo purposes

The biggest improvement in AI coding assistants doesn't come from better prompts. It comes from better context.

## Key points

- LLMs perform dramatically better when they have access to project history, decisions, and conventions
- A well-maintained knowledge base eliminates the need to repeat yourself across sessions
- The cost of maintaining context is far lower than the cost of re-explaining everything
- Context compounds over time: each session adds to the knowledge base, making the next session more productive

## Implications

Teams using persistent context report 3-5x fewer "hallucinated" suggestions from their AI tools. The AI stops guessing and starts referencing.

The shift is from "prompt engineering" to "context engineering" — designing the information environment around the AI, not crafting the perfect question.
